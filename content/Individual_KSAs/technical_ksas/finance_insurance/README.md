## Labor Market Context
*Populated by T-README-02 | Data source: BLS OES + O\*NET + sector workforce studies*

**BLS Total Employment (current):** 10,351,440 in Business and Financial Operations Occupations (SOC 13-0000, BLS OES May 2024). Finance and insurance industry (NAICS 52-53): approximately 8.9 million workers total, including financial specialists, clerks, managers, and service representatives. Key sub-groups: Accountants and auditors: ~1.5M; Financial and investment analysts: ~530K; Insurance underwriters: ~105K; Financial risk specialists: ~60K; Loan officers: ~320K; Personal financial advisors: ~300K.

**BLS 10-Year Projected Growth (% / absolute jobs):** Business and financial operations occupations overall: faster than average 2024–2034; 942,500 openings per year (BLS OOH). Finance and insurance industry: approximately +2–4% projected. Key sub-group projections: Personal financial advisors: +13%; Financial examiners: +20%; Financial analysts: +10%; Actuaries: +17%. RegTech and InsurTech roles growing faster than average. Insurance clerks and traditional bank teller roles declining.

**Median Annual Wage:** $80,920 (Business and Financial Operations Occupations group median, BLS OOH May 2024). By sub-group: Financial analysts: ~$99,890; Accountants and auditors: $79,880; Personal financial advisors: $99,580; Insurance underwriters: $79,900; Financial risk specialists: $112,100; Actuaries: $134,990.

**Common Entry-Level Titles:** Financial Analyst I, Junior Underwriter, Credit Analyst, Bank Examiner (entry), Compliance Associate, Insurance Sales Agent

**Common Mid-Career / Senior Titles:** Senior Financial Analyst, Portfolio Manager, Risk Manager, Chief Compliance Officer, Actuary, VP of Finance, InsurTech Product Manager

**Emerging / watch_2030 Roles:** RegTech Compliance Analyst (automated regulatory compliance monitoring), Digital Asset Custody Specialist (crypto/blockchain institutional finance), Sustainable Finance / ESG Reporting Analyst

**CSRA Employment Density:** Synovus Financial Corp (Columbus, GA; regional hub), First Bank of Georgia, South State Bank, multiple national bank branches (Wells Fargo, Bank of America, Truist). Estimated 4,500–6,000 finance and insurance professionals in Augusta-Richmond County MSA. Georgia statewide finance and insurance workers: ~255,000+ (BLS OES 2024 estimate).
