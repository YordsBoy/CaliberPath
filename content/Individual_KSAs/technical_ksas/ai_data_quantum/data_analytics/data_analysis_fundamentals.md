  - level: Basic

  - level: Intermediate
    indicator: Writes queries/scripts to aggregate data; applies measures such as variance,
      correlation, and basic hypothesis tests.
  - level: Advanced
    indicator: Conducts multivariate analyses, builds dashboards, and communicates insights to
      stakeholders.
  - level: Expert
    indicator: Designs complex analytical studies, mentors others in statistical reasoning, and
      governs analytical best practices organization-wide.
