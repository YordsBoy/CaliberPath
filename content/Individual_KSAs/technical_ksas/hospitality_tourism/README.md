Hospitality & Tourism
=====================

Travel demand rebounded to **96 % of 2019 levels** in 2024, yet guests now expect contact-free tech, wellness options, and verifiable sustainability. ISO 21401 and Global Sustainable Tourism Council (GSTC) standards guide greener operations. STR’s Certification in Hotel Industry Analytics (CHIA) remains the gold standard for revenue science, while HACCP and GBAC STAR protocols safeguard food and sanitation. UNWTO’s crisis toolbox helps properties build resilience amid climate events and geopolitical shocks. AHLEI curricula underpin core operational KSAs, from front office to housekeeping, and WTTC Safe-Travels plus WHO guidelines frame post-pandemic health measures.

**BLS Total Employment (current):** Food Preparation and Serving Related Occupations (SOC 35-0000): 13,005,810 (BLS OES May 2024). Lodging industry (NAICS 721): approximately 1.9 million workers. Accommodation and food services sector (NAICS 72) total: approximately 16.4 million workers. Key sub-groups: Food service workers/cooks: 3.1M; Fast food and counter workers: 4.1M; Waiters and waitresses: 2.5M; Lodging managers: 41,350; Concierges: 44,200; Meeting, convention, and event planners: 134,670.

**BLS 10-Year Projected Growth (% / absolute jobs):** Accommodation and food services industry: approximately +5% 2024–2034. Food service industry: faster than average driven by continued restaurant expansion and experiential dining demand. Private other schools and instruction (arts/recreation instruction): +7%. Hotel accommodation: +3–4%; tech adoption (mobile check-in, contactless) limiting some labor demand. Revenue management and digital marketing roles in hospitality: faster than average growth. Arts, entertainment, and recreation sector: +6.4% 2024–2034 (BLS).

**Median Annual Wage:** $29,120 (Food Preparation and Serving Related group median, BLS OOH May 2024); $77,460 (Lodging Managers, BLS OES May 2024); $65,090 (Meeting, Convention, and Event Planners, BLS OES May 2024); $43,090 (Tour and Travel Guides, BLS OES May 2024). Revenue management specialists: estimated $65,000–$95,000. Accommodation/food services industry mean annual wage: approximately $32,000–$38,000.
