Professional & Scientific Services
==================================

Management-consulting, analytics, and scientific-advisory firms drive **USD 1.2 trillion** in global revenue. Mastery now spans rigorous research ethics, EVMS-level project control, AI-enabled analytics, and ISO 56005 innovation-IP governance. PMI’s *Managing Change in Organizations* equips transformation projects. ANSI/EIA-748 and NDIA intent guides define earned-value best-practice. WIPO’s IP Strategy resources and ISO 56005 integrate innovation and protection. Publication integrity relies on COPE guidelines, while OECD reports set global research-integrity norms. AICPA SSCS No. 1 anchors consulting ethics, and GRI standards guide ESG advisory.

## Labor Market Context
*Populated by T-README-02 | Data source: BLS OES + O\*NET + sector workforce studies*

**BLS Total Employment (current):** Professional and Business Services sector: approximately 22.8 million workers (BLS QCEW 2024). Management Analysts (13-1111): ~875,000. Architecture and Engineering Occupations (SOC 17-0000): approximately 2.8 million. Professional, Scientific, and Technical Services industry (NAICS 54): approximately 10.2 million workers. Life, Physical, and Social Science Occupations (SOC 19-0000): ~1.4 million. Operations Research Analysts (15-2031): ~107,760.

**BLS 10-Year Projected Growth (% / absolute jobs):** Professional, scientific, and technical services industry: +7.5% 2024–2034 (BLS Employment Projections). Management analysts: +11% / +97,000 jobs. Operations research analysts: +21% / +22,700 jobs. Architecture and engineering occupations group: +7% (net, with high variation by specialty). Sustainability advisory and ESG consulting: fastest-growing consulting sub-segment, no distinct BLS code; estimated 20–25%+ growth driven by ISSB/TCFD disclosure requirements. Scientific R&D services industry: +8.7%.

**Median Annual Wage:** $99,310 (Management Analysts, BLS OES May 2024); $91,290 (Operations Research Analysts, BLS OES May 2024); $89,690 (Life, Physical, and Social Science Occupations group median, BLS OES May 2024); $90,890 (Architecture and Engineering Occupations group median, BLS OES May 2024). Management consulting median at senior levels: $120,000–$160,000+ (BLS management occupations data, plus sector supplemental data from McKinsey/Vault reports).
